## Raw-source artifacts

[`RAW_SOURCES.md`](RAW_SOURCES.md) is the artifact-level rights map. It records
the exact raw artifacts allowlisted for the separate public release bundle and
the notices and restrictions that accompany each copy. The public Git history
contains no binary raw artifacts. Every retained source outside that release
allowlist remains local.

Public availability, local retention, or citation in an `SRC` note is not by
itself permission to redistribute an artifact. A raw artifact may enter a
public release bundle only after an artifact-specific review confirms
permission for that exact version and all required notices accompany it.

Brief quotations and factual summaries in the wiki are provided for evidence,
criticism, review, and research context. They do not change the rights status of
the underlying work. Consult the relevant source and its terms before reusing
third-party material.
