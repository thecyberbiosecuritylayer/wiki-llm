# Animal and plant health assets and stakeholders

## Asset/stakeholder matrix

| Domain | Biological/material and production assets | Digital/record/system assets | Responsible and affected roles |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Animal health | terrestrial/kept/aquatic/aquaculture/wild animals, animal groups/epidemiological units, germinal products, products/by-products, establishments, transport, vaccines/diagnostic reagents and samples | identification, registration, movement, health-status, laboratory-result, certificate and traceability records; computerised databases/TRACES | operators, transporters, animal professionals, veterinarians, laboratory staff, competent authorities, Member States/Commission, animal/public-health interests |
| Plant health | seeds and other plants for planting, tissue culture/germplasm, plants/products, wood/packaging, lots/trade units, pests, production/storage/transport/quarantine objects | operator registers, traceability codes, survey/test/notification records, plant passports, certificates, EUROPHYT/TRACES records | professional/registered/authorised operators, final users, laboratories, competent authorities, inspectors, Member States/Commission and ecosystem/social/economic interests |

This map contains legal classes, not an inventory of a farm, herd, seed lot,
laboratory, system or person. U.S. Part 86 directly supplies the literal herd
class; EU group/epidemiological-unit, flock and group/lot classes remain
distinct. Sensor/equipment and genomic/sample evidence remains source-specific.

> **Claim record — AST-0009-C01 | source-report**
> **Claim:** The EU package directly maps seed/planting-material and animal-
> group/establishment/epidemiological-unit record classes together with
> material, sample, laboratory,
> movement, traceability, authority/operator and affected-health/ecosystem
> roles required to complement the existing agriculture asset envelope.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Direct EU legal asset/stakeholder classes; not a current local
> inventory, full genome model, deployed sensor system or complete AGE-AS pass.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-10-10.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** [SRC-0037](../sources/src-0037-eu-animal-plant-health-imsoc-legislative-ecosystem.md),
> `SRC-0037-C03/C05/C06`; relation locator above.
> **Basis / limits:** Classes are explicit. `Herd` is not an operative term
> and these classes are not equated with one universal herd record. One EU
> ecosystem is not SF2.
