# NIH Intramural Electronic Laboratory Notebook governance

## Instrument identity and current presentation

> **Claim record — GOV-0013-C01 | source-report**
> **Claim:** NIH OIR currently presents the assembled live material as the NIH
> IRP Electronic Lab Notebook Policy and directly links to/cross-references a
> Ninth Edition 2025 Conduct-of-Research companion amended May 2026.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Official institutional presentation checked 2026-07-12; not a
> claim that every platform statement on the 2024-updated page is current.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-08-11.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** [SRC-0023](../sources/src-0023-nih-intramural-electronic-laboratory-notebook-policy.md),
> `SRC-0023-C01/C09`; HTML title/introduction/footer and direct companion
> link; companion cover/Preface, physical pp. 1–2.
> **Basis / limits:** Current presentation, issuer and companion identity are
> direct. The page requires early recheck because its platform text is stale.

## Modality, force and population

> **Claim record — GOV-0013-C02 | analytic-judgment**
> **Claim:** The instrument is an internal NIH IRP institutional policy/
> guidance requirement for defined Notebook Owners and Users, not a statute,
> regulation, accreditation standard, general extramural NIH grant condition
> or universal laboratory rule; the source's two `draft guidance` labels remain
> an unresolved modality defect.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Instrument classification and population; not legal advice or a
> determination about separately incorporated federal-records requirements.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-08-11.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** [SRC-0023](../sources/src-0023-nih-intramural-electronic-laboratory-notebook-policy.md),
> `SRC-0023-C02`; HTML title/introduction and §§I, III.A–B; companion
> Introduction, printed p. 1/physical p. 4.
> **Basis / limits:** Requirement, institutional population and conflicting
> wording are literal. Wiki does not manufacture a cleaner modality.

> **Claim record — GOV-0013-C03 | source-report**
> **Claim:** NIH assigns research-record ownership to NIH, institutional
> access/control authority to NIH/IC leadership, day-to-day control and
> availability to responsible investigators, and authorized-use
> responsibilities to trainees/scientific staff within the defined IRP
> hierarchy.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Stated actor/responsibility allocation; not observed access,
> compliance, enforcement or a rule for non-NIH institutions.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-08-11.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** [SRC-0023](../sources/src-0023-nih-intramural-electronic-laboratory-notebook-policy.md),
> `SRC-0023-C05`; HTML §§III.A–B, IV.C and VI.A; companion printed
> pp. 5, 8–9/physical pp. 8, 11–12.
> **Basis / limits:** Actor allocation is direct; actual authorization and
> adherence are not measured.

## Governance and control modalities

> **Claim record — GOV-0013-C04 | source-report**
> **Claim:** The policy allocates governance requirements across complete
> electronic record keeping, attributable integrity history, authorization,
> retention, backup, institutional access and deployment authorization, with
> heightened capability selection where research context requires it.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Institutional governance/control design; not proof of product
> validation, deployment, compliance or effectiveness.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-08-11.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** [SRC-0023](../sources/src-0023-nih-intramural-electronic-laboratory-notebook-policy.md),
> `SRC-0023-C03/C05`–`C07`; HTML §§II, IV.C–D and VI.A–B; companion
> printed pp. 8–13/physical pp. 11–16.
> **Basis / limits:** Requirements and role allocation are direct. No completed
> control test or organization-wide adoption metric is supplied.
