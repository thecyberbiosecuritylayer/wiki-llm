# EU Animal Health, Plant Health and IMSOC legislation

## Modality, force and current state

| Instrument | Issuer/form | Current retained state | Scope/adoption boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Regulation (EU) 2016/429 | Parliament/Council regulation | in force; current EUR-Lex consolidation 2021-04-21; Article 283 application boundary | directly applicable animal-health design with species/disease/risk derogations and delegated/implementing dependencies |
| Regulation (EU) 2016/2031 | Parliament/Council regulation | in force; current consolidation 2026-07-06; Article 113 application boundary | directly applicable plant-health design with pest/object/operator/movement exceptions and dependent acts |
| Implementing Regulation (EU) 2019/1715 | Commission implementing regulation | in force; current consolidation 2021-12-01; Article 48 staged application | IMSOC procedures depend on parent animal/plant/official-control rules and Member-State authorities/systems |

Authentic Official Journal texts govern; consolidated texts are official
documentation tools without independent legal effect. The three instruments
are one EU ecosystem, not three jurisdictions or three SF2 lineages.

## Accountability architecture

Animal/plant operators, transporters and professional roles carry scoped
prevention, record, movement and notification duties. Laboratories and
competent authorities carry confirmation, reporting and control roles.
Member States register/approve actors, operate authorities/systems, impose
penalties and coordinate with the Commission. IMSOC assigns component-network,
contact-point, data-owner, controller, access, maintenance and certificate
responsibility.
