# Source title

## Bibliographic identity

Identify the work, authors or issuer, version, publication date, source type, and
canonical URL. Multiple representations of one work remain one source lineage.

## Provenance and accessible scope

Record the local artifact and hash, or say clearly that the source is external-
only. List unread figures, tables, appendices, attachments, or records. Do not
claim complete ingest when any material component was unavailable.
