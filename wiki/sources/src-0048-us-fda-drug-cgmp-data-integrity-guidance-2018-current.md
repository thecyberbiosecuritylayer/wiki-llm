## Data-integrity obligations and lifecycle

FDA cites requirements for exact/complete/secure backup, contemporaneous and
complete data, original/true-copy retention and record review. It defines
ALCOA, metadata and audit trails across creation, modification, processing,
maintenance, archival, retrieval, transmission and disposition.

> **Claim record — SRC-0048-C04 | source-report**

## Exceptions, enforcement context and adoption

Explicit bounded cases include shared read-only accounts only for viewing,
electronic signatures with controls, and static records only when they are the
original or a true copy; dynamic originals require preserved content/meaning.

industry-wide implementation measure.
