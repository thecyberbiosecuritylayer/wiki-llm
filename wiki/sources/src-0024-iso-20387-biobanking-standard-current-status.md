## Current published biobanking standard

> **Claim record — SRC-0024-C02 | source-report**
> **Claim:** ISO currently presents `ISO 20387:2018`, Edition 1, as a published
> 36-page International Standard at stage 90.92 (`to be revised`) covering
> competence, impartiality and consistent operation of research/development
> biobanks and quality of biological-material/data collections.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Official ISO identity/status/abstract as of 2026-07-12; not access
> to the 36-page normative text, certification or implementation.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-07-26.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** Main ISO metadata page, title/header/Abstract/General
> information/Life cycle: reference `ISO 20387:2018`, Edition 1, publication
> 2018-08, 36 pages, Published and stage 90.92.
> **Basis / limits:** Status and abstract are direct. `To be revised` does not
> mean withdrawn or already superseded.

> **Claim record — SRC-0024-C03 | source-report**
> **Claim:** The published abstract applies ISO 20387 to organizations
> biobanking human, animal, fungal, plant and microorganism material for
> research/development; it excludes food/feed production and therapeutic use
> and points diagnostic/treatment human-material entities first to ISO 15189
> and other clinical standards.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Public abstract-level scope/exclusions of the current 2018 edition;
> not a clause-level applicability or legal determination for an organization.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-07-26.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** Main ISO metadata page, Abstract and Notes 1–2.
> **Basis / limits:** Included/excluded classes are literal. Wiki does not infer
> an accreditation result or universal precedence from the abstract.

> **Claim:** ISO presents `ISO/FDIS 20387`, Edition 2, as a Final Draft under

> not yet an International Standard published at stage 60.60.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** ISO lifecycle state checked 2026-07-12; not a prediction of exact
> publication date or permission to apply draft wording as final requirements.
> **As of:** 2026-07-12.
> **Review after:** 2026-07-26.
> **Evidence status:** single-source
> **Confidence:** high
> **Evidence:** Edition 2 ISO metadata page, header/status/General information/
> Life cycle: Final Draft International Standard, Under development, Edition 2,
> stage 50.00 on 2026-06-22; publication stages 60.00/60.60 blank.
> **Basis / limits:** The ISO stage model is direct. Future replacement is not
> silently backdated into the cutoff.

> redline or current final requirements.
> **Claim status:** active
> **Scope:** Public-abstract comparison only; not full redline, final text or a

> **Review after:** 2026-07-26.
> **Evidence status:** single-source

> **Basis / limits:** Visible abstract differences are direct; no hidden clause
